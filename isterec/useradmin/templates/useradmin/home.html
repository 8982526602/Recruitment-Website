Welcome, {{ user.first_name }} {{ user.last_name }},


Instructions how to use:


  • To search through any SIG specific records click on the SIG specific link to your left.
    •
  • To view all applicants and their details of that particular SIG in a table form click on the search button without giving any search query.
  • To view details of a particular applicant enter his/her Name, Roll No, Mobile No, or email and click on the search button.
  • To view the list order by score click "order by score" button instead of the search button.
  • To view all applicants selected for next round click the selected button without giving a search query and to view a particular applicant enter his/her Name, Roll No, Mobile No, or email and click on the selected button.
  • To order by a particular attribute click on the attribute you want to order by in the table.
  • To read the responses/answers and complete details given by an applicant click on his/her Roll No in the table.
  • After reading his/her responses, to score a particular applicant enter a score in the range 1-10 and click on the save button to save it.
  • To select the applicant for next round check the check box named selected and click on the save button to save it.
